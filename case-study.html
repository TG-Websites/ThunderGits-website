01 — The Challenge

A leading biomass producer with zero digital footprint

Enersol Biopower, a Bihar-based manufacturer of high-quality biomass briquettes and pellets, had been serving industrial clients for years. Despite their product quality and capacity, they had no website, no digital catalogue, and relied entirely on word-of-mouth and trade networks for business.

They approached ThunderGits with a clear need: establish a professional digital presence that would allow them to showcase their product line, attract national and international buyers, and accept inquiries and orders online.

The key challenges were:

  • No existing brand identity or visual language
  • Complex product catalogue with multiple specifications
  • Need to appeal to both B2B buyers and retail customers
  • Tight delivery timeline of 8 weeks
02 — Our Approach

Strategy-first, then execution

We started with a brand discovery workshop to understand Enersol's vision, their customers, and competitors. From this, we defined a clean, professional visual identity — a green-and-amber palette representing sustainability and energy — that would stand out in the biomass sector.

For the e-commerce platform, we chose Next.js for its SEO advantages and fast rendering, paired with a Node.js + MongoDB backend to handle the product management system and inquiry flows. We built a custom admin dashboard so the Enersol team could manage products, orders, and customer queries without any technical knowledge.

The website was structured to serve both audiences: B2B buyers got detailed product specification sheets and a bulk inquiry form, while retail customers were guided through a streamlined checkout powered by Razorpay.

03 — The Solution

A complete digital ecosystem

We delivered a full-stack platform with the following capabilities:

  • Product Catalogue: Dynamic pages for each SKU with specs, certifications, and bulk pricing. Products managed entirely from the admin panel.
  • E-Commerce Checkout: Cart, order management, and Razorpay payment gateway for direct retail purchases.
  • B2B Inquiry System: A dedicated bulk inquiry form with email notifications to the sales team for every submission.
  • SEO Foundation: Meta tags, structured data, sitemap, and robots.txt configured to maximise indexation from day one.
  • Admin Dashboard: Role-based access for the Enersol team to manage products, view orders, and respond to inquiries.
04 — Results

Measurable impact from day one

Within 4 weeks of launch, Enersol Biopower saw a significant lift in inbound business inquiries directly attributed to the website. The platform began ranking on Google for several biomass product keywords in Bihar and Jharkhand.

The client team, with zero technical background, was able to independently add new products, update pricing, and respond to inquiries via the admin dashboard — achieving full operational independence from day one.

Most importantly, Enersol now has a brand presence that matches the quality of their product — a professional identity that instils confidence in both domestic and export buyers.

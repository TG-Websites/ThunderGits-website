Privacy Policy

ThunderGits Consultancy Services Pvt Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website thundergits.com. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data submitted through the website via:

  • Contact forms
  • Job application forms

Information includes your name, email, phone number, and service requirements.

2. Use of Information

The collected data is used internally to respond to your queries or evaluate job applications.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. All data is accessed only by our internal sales and HR teams.

4. Cookies

We do not use cookies or external analytics tools on this website.

5. Data Security

Your information is protected with access control mechanisms to ensure safety and privacy.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our website is safe for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect sensitive data from children.

7. Policy Changes

Since we do not register users, we may update this policy without individual notifications. Please check this page periodically.
